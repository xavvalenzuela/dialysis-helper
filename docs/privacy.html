Privacy Policy

Dialysis Helper  ·  Effective date: May 28, 2025

Dialysis Helper does not collect, transmit, or share any personal or health data. Everything stays on your device.

1. Overview

Dialysis Helper ("the App") is a personal health tracker designed for dialysis patients. It is developed and maintained by Xavier Valenzuela ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

The App does not:

  • Create or require a user account
  • Connect to the internet
  • Send any data to external servers
  • Use analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs
  • Share data with third parties

3. Data Stored on Your Device

All information you enter is stored locally on your device using an on-device SQLite database. This includes:

  • Your name (optional, used on exported PDFs)
  • Fluid intake logs
  • Pre- and post-dialysis weight logs
  • Blood pressure and pulse readings
  • Symptom notes
  • Uploaded documents (prescriptions, lab results, etc.)
  • App settings (fluid limit, notification preferences)

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly share or export it yourself (for example, by using the built-in share or PDF export features).

4. Notifications

The App can send a daily local reminder notification if you choose to enable it in Settings. This notification is scheduled entirely on your device and does not involve any external service. You can disable it at any time from Settings or from your device's notification settings.

5. Documents and Files

When you upload a document, the App copies the file into the App's private storage on your device. Files are never uploaded to a server. Exported PDF logs are also saved to your device's local storage. Sharing a file uses your device's standard share sheet — the App has no control over where you choose to send it.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
